Stray Dog

From numerous original songs, acoustic interpretations of classic rock tunes, 1940's jazz standards, all the way to bluegrass and blues, we put our own mark on a broad, eclectic mix of music. Eric's unique touch on the guitar over Joe's classy bass backs Anne's remarkably sweet, pure and honest voice, all while riding Zac's magical percussion on the cajon.
